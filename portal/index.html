How to Play

No downloads needed. Click Play Now, pick a team and ship, and you're in. Press ? in-game for the full key list.

Mouse

  • Left click — Set course
  • Right click — Fire torpedo
  • Middle click — Fire phaser

Weapons & Beams

  • w Torpedo · e Phaser · t Plasma
  • q Tractor beam · r Repressor
  • d Detonate enemy torps

Movement

  • 0–9 Set speed · !@# Speed 10/11/12
  • ←→ Turn · ↑↓ Speed up/down

Defense & Planet Ops

  • s Shields · f Cloak · g Repair
  • c Orbit · b Bomb · z/x Beam armies
